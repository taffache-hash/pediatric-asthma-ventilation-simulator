## Mandatory language before submission

Use:

> The simulator was internally verified for directional behavior.

Do not use:

> The simulator was clinically validated.

Use:

> Ketamine was represented as an optional conservative scenario modifier because pediatric evidence remains limited and mixed.

Do not use:

> Ketamine improves pediatric status asthmaticus in the model and therefore should be used clinically.
